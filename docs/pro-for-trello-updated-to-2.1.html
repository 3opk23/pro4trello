A quick FAQ

Let's try to answer a couple of repeating questions on the support board:

Why are Pro4Trello settings per board?

Trello is (amongst other things) designed for both teams and individuals. When you’re sharing your boards with other people you can’t always impose the way you form card/task titles to everyone. When you’re all alone, it makes sense to have unique settings for all Trello boards, but that just can’t work when you’re involved in a lot of teams with different approaches to organizing boards.

Why are time entries displayed with a calculator icon?

The clock icon is used by Trello for the due date/time. After some research on the topic and coming up with this term called "calculated time", the time entry label looked okay with the calculator icon.

What happened with your feature request?

Look, you've all been awesome at reviewing Pro for Trello, suggesting new features and (more often) pointing out it's flaws. The thing is - time is a valuable resource and right now it was used to fix, improve and optimize Pro for Trello. Please stay patient until the time comes for your feature request to be a part of one of the next versions. And rest assured it will.

If you think Pro4Trello is awesome - you can leave a rating in the Chrome Store. This will help other people learn about the extension. Also make sure to leave your feedback or a feature request!

Do you have a blog, a video channel or write for tech. media? Consider reviewing Pro for Trello in a video or an article. The author is always available for a comment or any kind of feedback.

2. Make a pull request
